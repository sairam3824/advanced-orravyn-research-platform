Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the platform
Who owns the research I upload?
You do. Users retain full ownership and control of the content they upload. By uploading, you grant the platform a non-exclusive license simply to store, display, and distribute the content for academic purposes on the Research Portal.
Is my personal data sold for advertising?
No, never. The platform is strictly non-commercial. No personal data is exploited for commercial gain or advertising. We do not include any third-party trackers or advertising integrations.
Can I delete my account and data?
Yes. You can request to delete your account, personal information, and any individual documents you have uploaded. Upon your request, the platform developers will proceed with the deletion of your data.
Can I use the platform for commercial purposes?
No. The Research Portal and all of its content are intended solely for non-commercial academic use. Any use for profit, resale, or business activity is strictly prohibited.
Is my data shared with third parties?
No. Your data is not shared with any third-party services without your explicit consent. The platform only uses essential third-party tools for infrastructure support and does not include any third-party trackers or advertising integrations.
What kind of content can I upload?
You are responsible for uploading only content that you own or have the explicit legal right to share. The content must not violate copyright, contain malicious code, or misrepresent academic authorship.
What technologies power this platform?
The platform is built using Django, Django REST Framework, PyTorch, TensorFlow, Sentence-Transformers, Elasticsearch, and more. The entire codebase is open-source under the Apache License 2.0. Visit our Open Source page for full details.
How do I report plagiarised or misused content?
Please email sairam.maruri@gmail.com with details about the content in question. We aim to review and respond to all reports within 72 hours.

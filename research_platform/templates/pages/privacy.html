Privacy Policy

Effective: August 4, 2025 Version 1.0 Next Review: August 8, 2026

Overview

The Research Portal is a student-developed, open-source academic platform created to facilitate research sharing and academic collaboration. It is strictly non-commercial and developed using open-source technologies. The platform provides an accessible environment for students, researchers, and educators to upload, explore, and collaborate on academic research content.

Purpose and Mission

The platform is intended solely for educational and academic purposes. It supports the ethical sharing of research materials and promotes open access to scholarly content. The mission is to enhance academic collaboration while safeguarding user data and respecting research integrity.

Data Collection and Use

User accounts may include data such as email addresses, usernames, affiliations, and optional profile information. This data is collected solely for authentication and academic networking purposes. User consent is required for data collection, and retention is limited to the active duration of the account plus one year.

Users may upload research papers along with associated metadata and version history. Ownership and control of uploaded content remain with the user. AI-powered features utilize anonymized search queries and behavioral data to improve recommendation systems.

Data Protection and Security

  • HTTPS is enforced across the entire website
  • Passwords are securely hashed
  • Access to administrative functionality is role-restricted
  • Encrypted storage is used for all documents
  • Code reviews are conducted regularly to ensure compliance

Privacy Principles

  • All data is used solely for educational purposes
  • No personal data is exploited for commercial gain or advertising
  • No third-party trackers or advertising integrations are included
  • Users have full control over document visibility
  • Users retain the right to access, correct, export, or delete their data at any time
  • Cookies are limited to essential session cookies required for secure login

User Rights and Access

Users can access and export all personal data and uploaded research materials. Assistance with data migration is available upon request. Users also have the ability to correct personal information, delete accounts, or remove individual documents.

International and National Compliance

The platform is designed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 of India.

Third-Party Services and Infrastructure

The platform uses open-source technologies including Django, MySQL, SQLite, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No user data is shared with any third-party services without explicit user consent.

Content Sourcing and Licensing

The Research Portal hosts academic research papers obtained exclusively from open-access repositories. The platform codebase is released under the Apache License 2.0.

Contact

For all privacy or platform-related inquiries, please contact: sairam.maruri@gmail.com. The standard response time is 48 to 72 hours.

About the Platform

orravyn.cloud Open Source

Project Overview

The Orravyn Research Portal is a student-developed, open-source academic platform designed to facilitate research sharing and collaboration among students, researchers, and educators. The mission is to enhance academic collaboration while safeguarding user data and respecting research integrity. The platform is provided "as is" and intended strictly for educational and non-commercial use.

What We Offer

AI-Powered Discovery

Advanced search and recommendation systems powered by NLP and transformer models to surface the most relevant research for your interests.

Research Groups

Create and join research groups, collaborate in real-time, and share papers with your peers across the globe.

Yggdrasil AI Assistant

An intelligent chatbot designed to help you navigate research papers, answer academic questions, and summarize content.

Privacy by Design

No third-party trackers, no advertising. All data handling is transparent and compliant with GDPR and Indian data protection law.
